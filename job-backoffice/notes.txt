Company Owner (Strategic Role)
  - Purpose: Manages the company account, not daily hiring.
  - Should be able to:
    - Create / edit company profile
    - Manage subscription & billing
    - Invite / remove team members
    - Assign roles (Hiring Manager, Recruiter)
    - View all jobs & reports
    - Not required to review every application
Think of them as:
“Admin of the company account”

Hiring Manager (Decision Maker)
  - Purpose: Owns the job position.
  - Should be able to:
    - Create job posts
    - Edit/delete their jobs
    - View applications for their jobs
    - Shortlist / reject candidates
    - Move candidates between stages
    - Final hiring decision
Think of them as:
“Owner of a job position”

Recruiter (Execution Role)
  - Purpose: Manages the recruitment process.
  - Should be able to:
    - Source candidates
    - Screen resumes
    - Schedule interviews
    - Communicate with candidates
    - Provide feedback to hiring manager
  - Not authorized to make final hiring decisions
Think of them as:
“Recruitment specialist”
