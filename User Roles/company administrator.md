# 2. Company Owner

## 2.1 Role Definition
The Company Owner (also referred to as Company Administrator) is the primary stakeholder representing an organization on the platform. They have the highest level of administrative access within their company's account and are responsible for overall recruitment strategy, team management, and business operations on the platform.

## 2.2 User Profile Characteristics

### Demographics
- **Role:** CEO, Founder, HR Director, VP of People Operations  
- **Company Size:** Small to medium businesses (10-500 employees), primary focus on 10-100 employee companies  
- **Age Range:** 30-55 years old  
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree or higher, often MBA or relevant business degree  
- **Technical Proficiency:** Moderate to high; comfortable with business software but not necessarily technical  
- **Industry:** Technology, business services, professional services, healthcare, finance, retail  

### Behavioral Traits
- Strategic thinkers focused on business growth  
- Budget-conscious with clear ROI expectations  
- Delegators who empower their teams  
- Data-driven decision makers  
- Brand-conscious about employer reputation  
- Time-constrained with competing priorities  
- Quality-focused over quantity in hiring  

### Persona Example (Lisa - HR Director)
- 42 years old  
- 15 years HR experience  
- Manages 3-person HR team  
- Responsible for 50+ hires annually  
- Moderate technical skills  
- Values efficiency and collaboration tools  
- Concerned with employer branding and compliance  

## 2.3 Core Objectives & Goals

### Primary Goals
- **Attract Quality Talent:** Build strong employer brand and attract qualified candidates  
- **Manage Recruitment Operations:** Oversee all hiring activities across departments  
- **Optimize Hiring Efficiency:** Reduce time-to-hire and cost-per-hire  
- **Enable Team Collaboration:** Provide tools for hiring managers to work effectively  
- **Monitor Performance:** Track recruitment metrics and ROI  
- **Ensure Compliance:** Maintain adherence to employment laws and company policies  

### Secondary Goals
- Build talent pipeline for future needs  
- Improve candidate experience and employer reputation  
- Standardize hiring processes across organization  
- Generate recruitment reports for leadership  
- Control platform costs and subscriptions  
- Integrate recruitment with broader HR systems  

### Business Objectives
- Reduce recruitment costs by 30%  
- Decrease time-to-hire by 25%  
- Improve quality of hire metrics  
- Increase offer acceptance rates  
- Build sustainable talent pipeline  
- Enhance employer brand perception  

## 2.4 Pain Points & Challenges

### Operational Challenges
- **Visibility Gap:** Difficulty tracking all hiring activities across multiple hiring managers  
- **Inconsistent Processes:** Different managers using different approaches leads to inefficiency  
- **Manual Reporting:** Time-consuming to compile recruitment metrics and reports  
- **Access Control:** Managing team permissions and ensuring data security  
- **Budget Constraints:** Limited resources for recruitment technology and advertising  
- **Integration Complexity:** Existing HR systems don't connect well with recruitment tools  

### Strategic Challenges
- **Employer Brand Management:** Maintaining consistent company image across job postings  
- **Talent Competition:** Competing with larger companies for same candidates  
- **Quality vs. Volume:** Balancing need for many hires with maintaining quality standards  
- **Remote Work Trends:** Adapting recruitment strategy for hybrid/remote positions  
- **Compliance Risks:** Ensuring all hiring practices meet legal requirements (EEO, GDPR, etc.)  
- **Succession Planning:** Building internal talent pipeline alongside external recruitment  

### Technical Pain Points
- Learning curves for new recruitment software  
- Data migration from previous systems  
- Limited technical support from vendors  
- Difficulty customizing systems to company needs  
- Poor mobile experience for on-the-go management  

## 2.5 System Access & Permissions

### Access Level
- **Administrator (Full Access)**  

### Capabilities

#### Company Management
- Create and fully manage company profile  
- Edit company information (name, industry, size, locations)  
- Upload and update company logo and branding assets  
- Write and edit company description and culture information  
- Add multiple office locations with details  
- Manage company social media links  
- Configure employer branding settings  
- Set up custom career page (future feature)  
- Verify company identity for badges  

#### Team & Access Management
- Invite unlimited team members via email  
- Assign roles: Admin, Hiring Manager, Recruiter, Viewer  
- Edit user roles and permissions at any time  
- Deactivate or remove team members  
- View activity logs for all team members  
- Set up approval workflows (future feature)  
- Manage department structures and hierarchies  
- Create custom permission sets (future feature)  

#### Job Posting Management
- Create new job postings  
- Edit all job postings (including those created by others)  
- Duplicate and reuse job templates  
- Publish, pause, or close any job posting  
- Delete draft job postings  
- Set job posting budgets and promotion levels  
- Configure application deadlines  
- Manage job posting visibility settings  
- Bulk actions on multiple postings  

#### Applicant & Candidate Management
- View all applications across all job postings  
- Access complete applicant profiles and submissions  
- Review resumes and attached documents  
- Filter and search all applicants company-wide  
- Change applicant status in pipeline  
- Add notes and ratings to any applicant  
- Assign applicants to team members for review  
- Message candidates directly  
- Bulk actions on applicants (status changes, messages)  
- Export applicant data  
- Archive or delete applicant records (with retention policies)  

#### Analytics & Reporting
- Access comprehensive analytics dashboard  
- View company-wide recruitment metrics:  
  - Total applications received  
  - Active job postings  
  - Time-to-hire averages  
  - Cost-per-hire  
  - Application sources and channels  
  - Conversion rates through pipeline  
  - Team performance metrics  
- Generate custom reports  
- Export reports to PDF and CSV  
- Schedule automated report delivery  
- Compare metrics across time periods  
- View hiring manager performance data  
- Track diversity and inclusion metrics (future feature)  

#### Communication & Collaboration
- Send messages to candidates from any job posting  
- Create and manage message templates  
- View all message history across team  
- Set up automated email workflows (future feature)  
- Manage notification settings for entire company  
- Broadcast announcements to team members  

#### Billing & Subscription
- View current subscription plan and usage  
- Upgrade or downgrade subscription tier  
- Add payment methods and manage billing  
- View invoice history  
- Set up billing notifications and alerts  
- Manage subscription renewals  
- Add or remove job posting credits  
- Purchase add-on features  

#### Settings & Configuration
- Configure company-wide settings  
- Manage notification preferences for team  
- Set up custom application forms (future feature)  
- Configure integrations with other systems  
- Manage API access keys (future feature)  
- Set data retention and privacy policies  
- Configure compliance settings (EEO, GDPR)  
- Customize workflow stages and statuses  
- Set up automated actions and triggers (future feature)  

#### Support & Administration
- Access priority customer support  
- Submit support tickets  
- Request account assistance  
- Access admin training materials  
- Provide feedback on platform features  
- Request new features or customizations  

### Restrictions
- Cannot access other companies' data  
- Cannot view personal information of job seekers who haven't applied  
- Cannot modify platform-wide settings or features  
- Cannot access system-level administration tools  
- Cannot directly modify billing transactions (must contact support)  

## 2.6 User Journey & Workflows

### Initial Onboarding Journey
**Registration → Company Setup → Team Invitation → First Job Posting → Application Review → Team Training**  

### Detailed Onboarding Workflow

#### Week 1: Account Setup

##### Registration (Day 1)
- Receive invitation or self-register  
- Verify business email address  
- Complete company registration form  
- Set up admin password and security settings  
- Accept terms of service and privacy policy  

##### Company Profile Creation (Days 1-2)
- Upload company logo  
- Fill in company details (name, industry, size, locations)  
- Write company description and values statement  
- Add office locations with maps  
- Link social media profiles  
- Add company benefits and perks  
- Submit for verification (optional but recommended)  

##### Billing Setup (Day 2)
- Select subscription plan  
- Add payment method (credit card or invoice)  
- Configure billing preferences  
- Set up budget alerts  

##### Team Onboarding (Days 2-3)
- Invite HR team members  
- Invite hiring managers across departments  
- Assign appropriate roles to each member  
- Set up notification preferences  
- Schedule training sessions for team  

# Week 2: First Job Postings

## 5. Template Creation (Days 4-5)
- **Review sample job posting templates**
- **Customize templates for company needs**
- **Create department-specific templates**
- **Set up screening question banks**

## Initial Job Postings (Days 5-7)
- **Work with hiring managers to identify open positions**
- **Create 3-5 initial job postings**
- **Configure application settings for each**
- **Set up application routing and notifications**
- **Publish job postings**
- **Share job links on company website and social media**

## Key Workflow Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Managing High-Volume Hiring
- Identify urgent hiring needs across departments
- Prioritize positions by business impact
- Create multiple job postings simultaneously
- Assign hiring managers to each position
- Set up automated screening workflows
- Monitor application volume and quality
- Adjust posting visibility and promotion
- Coordinate interview scheduling across team
- Track progress against hiring goals

### Scenario 2: Team Performance Management
- Review team member activity logs
- Identify bottlenecks in review process
- Provide coaching to underperforming managers
- Recognize high-performing team members
- Adjust role assignments and workload
- Conduct training sessions on best practices
- Implement process improvements

### Scenario 3: Budget and ROI Analysis
- Review monthly platform costs
- Analyze job posting performance vs. cost
- Calculate cost-per-hire for each position
- Compare to industry benchmarks
- Identify opportunities for optimization
- Adjust subscription tier or job posting credits
- Present ROI report to executive team

### Scenario 4: Compliance and Audit
- Review all active job postings for compliance
- Ensure EEO statements are included
- Audit applicant data handling practices
- Verify team member access levels
- Review data retention policies
- Document recruitment procedures
- Prepare for external audits if needed

## 2.7 Success Metrics & KPIs

### Individual Performance Metrics
- **Company profile completion percentage** (target: 100%)
- **Team members invited and active** (target: 100% of hiring team)
- **Average response time to applications** (target: <24 hours)
- **Job posting quality score** (internal rating system)
- **Team satisfaction with platform** (target: 4.5/5)

### Business Impact Metrics

#### Recruitment Efficiency
- **Time-to-hire:** Baseline 42 days → Target 32 days (25% reduction)
- **Cost-per-hire:** Target 30% reduction from previous solution
- **Application completion rate:** Target >80%
- **Offer acceptance rate:** Target >70%

#### Quality of Hire
- **New hire performance ratings** (90-day evaluation)
- **New hire retention at 6 months and 12 months**
- **Hiring manager satisfaction scores**
- **Candidate quality ratings**

#### Platform Utilization
- **Active job postings per month**
- **Applications received per posting**
- **Team member engagement** (logins per week)
- **Feature adoption rates** (collaboration tools, analytics)
- **ROI on platform investment**

#### Employer Brand
- **Company profile views**
- **Job posting view-to-application rate**
- **Candidate satisfaction scores** (post-application surveys)
- **Glassdoor rating correlation** (if applicable)

### Company-Wide KPIs
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) contribution**
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**
- **Net Promoter Score** (target: 50+)
- **Customer satisfaction (CSAT) score** (target: 4.5/5)

## 2.11 Compliance & Legal Responsibilities

### Data Protection Obligations
- Ensure GDPR compliance for EU applicants
- Maintain CCPA compliance for California applicants
- Implement data retention policies (typically 1-2 years)
- Provide data access and deletion upon request
- Secure storage of applicant personal information
- Document data processing agreements

### Employment Law Compliance
- Include EEO statements in job postings
- Avoid discriminatory language in job descriptions
- Maintain audit trails for hiring decisions
- Document reasonable accommodation requests
- Comply with wage transparency laws (where applicable)
- Follow record-keeping requirements (EEOC, OFCCP)

### Platform Usage Policies
- Ensure team members follow terms of service
- Monitor for policy violations
- Report and address inappropriate content
- Maintain professional communications with candidates
- Respect candidate privacy and consent
